## App Description

### Short Description (30 characters)
Get personalized college advice

### Full Description
Wizzmo connects college students with experienced mentors for personalized advice and guidance. Whether you're navigating course selection, career planning, or campus life, our community of verified student mentors is here to help.

**Key Features:**
• **Ask Questions**: Get personalized advice on academics, career planning, campus life, and more
• **Real-time Chat**: Connect instantly with mentors through text and voice messaging
• **Expert Mentors**: Access verified student mentors from top universities
• **Multiple Categories**: Find help in academics, career guidance, social life, and mental health
• **Anonymous Option**: Ask sensitive questions anonymously for privacy
• **Trending Topics**: Browse popular questions and advice from the community
• **Smart Matching**: Get connected with mentors who match your needs
• **Free Trial**: Try the service with free questions before subscribing

**Perfect for:**
- High school students planning for college
- Current college students seeking guidance
- Students navigating campus life and academics
- Anyone looking for peer-to-peer mentorship

Join thousands of students who trust Wizzmo for reliable, personalized college advice. Download now and get your first questions answered free!
